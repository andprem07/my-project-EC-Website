Frequently Asked

Questions commercial owners and retail partners ask.

What licensing do you hold?
Envision Contracting holds a California CSLB General B license and a Nevada State Contractors Board license. We also hold Plumbing and Electrical specialty classifications across multiple additional states. License numbers and specifics relevant to your project are available on request, and every license is verifiable via the issuing state board.
What does “one-stop shop” actually mean?
Architects, engineers, and surveyors supporting every bid, plus licensed general construction, self-performed plumbing, specialty trades on request, warehousing, last-mile delivery, installation crews, and property management — all under one accountable team. You sign one contract and you have one accountable team for the life of the project.
Where do you operate?
Commercial construction in California and Nevada. Retail logistics, warehousing, and installation programs across California, Arizona, Nevada, Texas, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, and Kentucky — with Hawaii, Oklahoma, Washington, and Oregon launching next.
Can you scale crews for peak retail season?
Yes. Our logistics division is built around skills-based dispatch and a vetted crew network across 12 states (and growing), so we flex up for peak windows without breaking the customer experience.
Do you offer property management for the assets you build?
Yes. We offer ongoing property management for commercial assets we build, so owners can keep one accountable team from groundbreaking through long-term operations.

Need a commercial bid or a multi-state logistics partner?

Send us your project, territory, timeline, and required scope. We’ll route the inquiry to the right division and follow up within one business day.
