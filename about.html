Envision Contracting.

Licensed General B commercial contractor and multi-state logistics operator. Envision Contracting was established in 2019, building on more than two decades of industry experience by founder Andy Mikayelyan and the team behind the Premier brand. Construction and logistics under one licensed team.

Our Story

Two decades in the industry. One accountable brand.

Envision Contracting was established in 2019 to consolidate decades of commercial-construction and final-mile logistics work into one accountable brand. Founder Andy Mikayelyan got his start in the industry in 2003, then built and ran the Premier brand — Premier General Contract Group on the construction side and Premier Logistics & Transportation on the delivery side — into a multi-state operator serving custom home builders and national retailers across California, Nevada, and Arizona.

Today, Envision Contracting brings licensed General B construction together with architects, engineers, surveyors, self-performed plumbing, specialty classifications, warehousing, last-mile delivery, installation, and property management — all under one accountable team. We’ve been serving the same kinds of clients in two ways for two decades. Now we sell that capability as one company.

Founder

Built by an operator. Run like one.

Envision Contracting is built and led by Andy Mikayelyan — a hands-on operator with more than 20 years in the industry.

Andy Mikayelyan

Founder & CEO

Andy founded Envision Contracting on a simple operating principle: own the field, own the outcome. He started in the industry in 2003 running last-mile delivery and installation crews in Northern California, then scaled into programs for national retailers and custom home builders across California, Nevada, and Arizona.

In 2015 he opened a dedicated training school to develop installers in-house — a model Envision continues today as the backbone of multi-state field quality.

Andy holds a Juris Doctor from McGeorge School of Law and a Bachelor of Arts in Economics. Outside work, he’s a proud father of three.
